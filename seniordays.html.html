My Senior High School Days

My senior year of high school or grade 11, as the COVID-19 pandemic was still preventing us from going outside. However, I still went through homeschooling like everyone else, which is really depressing. When the government finally declared that we could attend school, I was happy because I could spend time with my friends and, most importantly, I could learn in class, but the switch from homeschooling to formal education following the pandemic was undoubtedly difficult. I had to make several social and academic modifications as a result. I had to adjust to going to classes in person once more, which required me to adhere to a routine and communicate with my peers every day. Furthermore, I had to gained a better understanding of my identity and life goals. It was a difficult yet significant time that helped mold me into the person I am now. All things considered, it was a challenging but essential shift that aided in my development and ability to adjust to shifting conditions.
